## FACEME | A VISUAL OF FACEBOOK PROFILE
• A webapp to display users’ facebook data in an interesting way.
• Shows who loves you most, when are you most active on social media,
how is your timeline divided and other insights.
• Made using: d3.js, Django, Python, facebook graph api
