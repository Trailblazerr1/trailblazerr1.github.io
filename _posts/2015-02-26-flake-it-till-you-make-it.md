**Scene**:
A rich kid who loves to write stories is reading a letter. His servant's son (also  his best friend) is organising his room.  

_Rich Kid_: "My uncle liked my story. He wrote 'Bravo!'"

_Friend_: "Well of course he liked it. You tell great stories. 
              What's the story about?"

_Rich Kid_: "It's about a very poor man who finds a magic cup. Soon he learns if he weeps into the cup, his tears would turn to pearls….And at the end of story, he's sitting on a mountain of pearls with a bloody knife in his hands and his dead wife in his arms. "

_Friend_: "So he killed her so that he could cry and get rich?"

_Rich Kid_: "Yes, you're very quick"

_Friend_: "But why couldn't he just smell an onion?"
